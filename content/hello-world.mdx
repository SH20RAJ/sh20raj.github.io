Hi there! I'm Shaswat Raj (sh20raj), and this is the first post of many. For a long time, I've shared my thoughts on Dev.to (to over 45k+ followers!), but I realized I needed a space I fully controlled—a digital garden where my research, experiments, and career heritage can live together.

### What is the Goal?
This blog isn't just about tutorials. It's about the **"Why"** behind the code. I plan to document:
- **Case Studies**: Deep dives into how I build systems like ResumeFlow or my startup projects.
- **Architectural Rants**: Why certain patterns (like the recent shift in Next.js params) matter.
- **Open Source Philosophy**: Tips on maintaining scale while staying sane.
- **Career Heritage**: Reflections on my journey from Class 7 to leading technical teams.

### The Tech Behind the Scenes
I believe in "Eating your own dog food." This blog is built with:
- **Next.js 16**: Utilizing the latest in static site generation.
- **MDX**: Allowing me to embed interactive React components directly in my posts.
- **Velite**: A powerful content layer that ensures my blog posts are type-safe and performant.

### Join the Conversation
I've always believed that coding is a social activity. Whether you're here to learn about System Design or just curious about my local project dump, I hope you find something that sparks your curiosity.
