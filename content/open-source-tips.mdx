Maintaining 40+ repositories has taught me that consistency and documentation are key to community growth.

### My Top 3 Tips:
1. **Automate everything** with GitHub Actions.
2. **Prioritize documentation** so users can help themselves.
3. **Be responsive** to issues and PRs.

Happy coding!
